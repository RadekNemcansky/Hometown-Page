Luzanky Park

Cathedral of Saints Peter and Paul

which is a Roman Catholic cathedral located on the Petrov hill in the Brno-střed district. It is commonly referred to locally as simply "Petrov"

Jakubske Namesti

The square, often simply called Jakubák, serves as the centre of Brno's social life, with its pulsating, cosmopolitan atmosphere, enlivened by a number of pubs, cafés, and bars
